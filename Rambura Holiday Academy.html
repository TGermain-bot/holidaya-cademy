About Us

Rambura Holiday Academy is a seasonal coaching school located in Rambura Sector. We offer academic support and skill development programs during school holidays to help students grow, succeed, and build a stronger future.

Ibyerekeye Ishuri

Ishuri rya Rambura Holiday ni ishuri ry'igihe cy'ibiruhuko riherereye mu Murenge wa Rambura. Ritanga ubufasha mu myigire n'amasomo ateza imbere ubumenyi bw'abanyeshuri mu gihe cy'ibiruhuko kugira ngo bazagire ejo hazaza heza kandi hafatika.
